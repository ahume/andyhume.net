--- layout: post title: The stuff I used to do published: false meta: _edit_last: "2" tags: [] type: post status: draft --- A few people recently have been asking me about my past life as a musician. This seems to be because I wrote a few words about it in my about section, because I rarely talk about it at work or anywhere else. I also write a post a few weeks ago about Michael Tippett. The answer that satisfies most people is 'Yes, I really was a professional orchestral musician'. Actually, that sounds weird even to me now. What I find more odd however, is how little people know what a job like that might actually entail on a day-to-day basis. There are lots of really good and bad things about being a musician.
