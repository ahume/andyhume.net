About Andy Hume

I build web sites and web applications. I currently work for the Guardian in London in the software architecture team.

Over the years I've written code and consult for clients like the BBC, Channel 4 and NBC. In our spare time we built the award winning font embedding platform Fontdeck.

Once upon a time I worked for Multimap where I led development of their JavaScript-driven mapping site. After Multimap was acquired by Microsoft I worked as Development Lead for Bing Maps UK, managing an engineering team on products serving 30 million users across both LAMP and .NET platforms.

Over the years I've worked on projects for Microsoft, the BBC, NBC, Mozilla, Yell, BT, Sony, Gumtree, and many others.
